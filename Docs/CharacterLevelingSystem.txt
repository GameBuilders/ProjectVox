Stats:
	Strength (Str) - Physical damage, attack/movement energy usage discount
	Intelligence (Int) - Non-physical ability damage, skill energy usage discount
	Constitution (Con) - Determines max health
	Dexterity (Dex) - Ranged and sneak damage, sneak crit multiplier
